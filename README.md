# Cat Cafe ᓚᘏᗢ

A simple, low-poly, Cat Cafe! 
You play as Catt, a cat who runs a cafe in rural Myau. Your goal is to serve customers and obtain coins to
save up to move and a beach house and buy an unlimited supply of fish to live a peaceful and carefree life.

This game was made using Godot and free assets from Tiny Treats.
Made for Athena Award's Sleepover!
